About me

Hello! I am a PhD student in the Chester F. Carlson Center for Imaging Science at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) in Rochester, NY. I currently work in the Machine and Neuromorphic Perception Laboratory (a.k.a Klab) under the supervision of Dr. Christopher Kanan.
