About me

Hello! I am a PhD student in the Chester F. Carlson Center for Imaging Science at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) in Rochester, NY. I currently work in the Machine and Neuromorphic Perception Laboratory (a.k.a Klab) under the supervision of Dr. Christopher Kanan. Previously, I worked as a Data Scientist at Everis Peru working on computer vision and recommendation systems. Before that, I worked as a Machine Learning Research Intern at Siemens Healthineers working on medical imaging and deep learning.

My research interests include self-supervised learning, lifelong machine learning, and computer vision.
